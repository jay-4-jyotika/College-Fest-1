About Fest Name

Fest Name the annual cultural and tech fest of Indian Institute of Information Technology Kalyani is about to see its beginning this year. It will be held from Januray 26th till January 28th. It is an inimitable product of West Bengal’s unique lust for life and the student gymkhana’s proverbial tendency towards excellence.

We are young, wild, free and exuberant. Come join us in this 3 day extravaganza as we celebrate and indulge in the joys of life.

Mark your calendars, and come to Fest Name to win prizes, make new friends, and let yourself loose!
